Frequently Asked Questions about Ijen Bromo Tour

How to go to Mount Ijen from Bali?

Most travelers take a ferry to Java and continue by car or transfer service to Ketapang, then on to Banyuwangi near Ijen.

What is the best time to visit Bromo?

The best time is during the dry season from April to October for clearer skies and cooler mornings.

Is Ijen safe for beginners?

Yes, with a guide and proper preparation, Ijen is accessible for first-time hikers.

How many days for Ijen Bromo tour?

A 2-3 day itinerary lets you visit both Ijen and Bromo comfortably with time for waterfalls.
